# Muscle Daddies Privacy Policy

**Effective date:** February 7, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Muscle Daddies (“we,” “us,” or “our”) collects, uses, shares, and protects information when you use the Muscle Daddies mobile application (the “App”).

If you have questions, contact us at **support@muscledaddiesapp.net**.

## 1. Who We Are
**Controller:** Thomas Martin
**Contact:** support@muscledaddiesapp.net

## 2. Information We Collect
We collect the following categories of information:

### 2.1 Account Information
- Display name
- Sign‑in credentials provided by Apple Sign‑In (token/identifier)

### 2.2 Health and Fitness Data (Optional)
If you connect Apple Health (HealthKit), we may read:
- Workouts, duration, distance, and energy burned
- Heart rate metrics (including HRV, resting heart rate, and heart rate recovery)
- Sleep and mindfulness summaries

We **only read** HealthKit data; we do not write to HealthKit.

### 2.3 Activity and Progress Data
- Workouts you log manually
- Stats (e.g., strength/speed/endurance/recovery), XP, and level
- Streaks, achievements, and challenges

### 2.4 Community/Group Data
If you join a group, we store and display:
- Your display name and workout feed items
- Likes, comments, and challenge participation

### 2.5 Device and App Data
- Basic app diagnostics needed to provide the service

## 3. How We Use Information
We use your information to:
- Provide and personalize the App experience
- Calculate stats, XP, recovery, and streaks
- Power social features such as group feeds, comments, and challenges
- Improve reliability and fix bugs

## 4. How We Share Information
We do **not** sell your personal information.

We may share limited information in these cases:

### 4.1 Group Sharing (Optional)
If you join a group, your workouts, stats, and feed activity can be visible to other members of that group.

### 4.2 Service Providers
We use third‑party infrastructure to operate the App, including Firebase (Firestore/Authentication). These providers process data on our behalf under their own terms and security practices.

### 4.3 Legal Requirements
We may disclose information if required by law or to protect the rights and safety of users or others.

## 5. Health Data and Privacy
- Health data is only accessed if you grant permission.
- You can revoke access at any time in iOS Settings.
- We only read data from Apple Health; we do not write data to it.

## 6. Location Data
- Route/location data is kept **on device** unless you explicitly share it.
- Shared workout information is visible to members of your group as described above.

## 7. Data Retention
We retain your data while your account is active. You may request deletion by contacting us at **support@muscledaddiesapp.net**.

## 8. Children’s Privacy
The App is intended for users **13 and older**. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us personal data, contact us to remove it.

## 9. Your Choices
- Connect or disconnect Apple Health at any time
- Edit your profile information
- Leave groups to stop sharing with them
- Request account deletion

## 10. Security
We use reasonable administrative and technical safeguards to protect your data. No system is 100% secure, but we take data protection seriously.

## 11. Changes to This Policy
We may update this policy. If we make material changes, we will update the effective date above.
